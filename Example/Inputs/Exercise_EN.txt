Exercise 1 – Weekly Study Tracking for 5 Subjects
A student wants to analyze how many hours they studied during a week. They track study time for 5 subjects during 7 days. Write a C program that:
1.	Input / Stores the study hours in a 5×7 matrix.
2.	Prints: The full matrix and the total study hours per subject.
3.	Uses the following functions:
•	loadMatrix → procedure to loads all values into the 5×7 matrix
•	printMatrix → procedure to print the matrix on the screen
•	sumRow → returns the total hours studied for one subject across the whole week
